How It All Started

Owner

I started baking since I was in High School, but did not make it into a business until I was in College, which I did part-time.

My Homebased Bakeshop came in full term in 2015. At first, I started with cupcakes that my friends offered their friends & colleagues.

Then I ventured into making cakes, themed cakes & wedding cakes.




Bakeshop

Homebased Bakeshop started in 2015.
